Research Interests

📡

Hyperspectral Change Detection

Morphological profiles, tensor decomposition, and deep learning for detecting changes in hyperspectral imagery.

🎯

Hyperspectral Anomaly Detection

Collaborative representation, subspace methods, and spectral-spatial fusion for detecting anomalies without prior information.

👁

Hyperspectral Object Tracking

Siamese networks, tensor sparse correlation filters, and band attention grouping for hyperspectral video tracking.

📊

Image Quality Assessment

Evaluating and enhancing quality of remote sensing and infrared imagery.
