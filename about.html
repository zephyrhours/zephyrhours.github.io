Curriculum Vitae.

Zengfu Hou (侯增福)

My background is photogrammetry and remote sensing, and I'm about to get the Ph.D. from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT). I'm now employed by Beijing Hornor Terminal Co., Ltd. as a senior engineer in graphics and image algorithms. My research interests are pattern recognition and hyperspectral image processing, including anomaly detection, target detection, change detection, object tracking, and image quality assessment, etc..
